F&F Return Policy

Orders Less Than $50

A non-refundable $50 shipping and handling fee is charged to customers who are signed into their f&f.com account when they place an order totaling less than $50 (before tax) that is shipped to one address within the contiguous United States. This $5 shipping and handling fee will not be charged for items that incur an associated delivery surcharge as described above.

Orders of $50+

Except for certain exceptions noted below, no shipping and handling is charged to customers who are signed into their Zoro.com account for standard ground delivery on orders of $50 or more before tax and shipped to one address within the continental United States.

Please note: Customers who check out as a guest with an order less than $75 before tax will have shipping and handling charges added to their order.

Carriers

We use a variety of carriers for shipments. Title and risk of loss pass to customer upon tender of shipment to carrier.

Third-Party and Manufacturer Shipped Products

Items shipping from the manufacturer: Please refer to the item page on f&f.com for shipping lead times as they vary. Expedited shipping is NOT available for third-party items.

When your order is shipped you will receive an email with a tracking number that links to the carrier's website.

Non-Refundable Items

Sometimes products may be non-refundable. If you order a non-refundable product, we will contact you prior to fulfilling your order, advise you of the refund restrictions, and offer you the option to cancel.

Call Customer Service

You can call our Customer Service Department at (419) 524-1700 to help walk you through the return process. We’ll send you a shipping label and a return authorization (RA) number. Our customer service hours are Monday through Friday from 8am to 5pm (CT)
